Introduction


As retired physicians, you've contributed mightily to society during your careers. Still, many of you long to continue making a difference.

The National Association of Retired Physicians (NARP) was founded to help you do just that. Our goal is to give you the opportunity to:

  • Mentor practicing physicians, residents and fellows Join Now
  • Share your knowledge
  • Have a free-flowing dialogue with other retired physicians Join now
  • Help change legal restrictions so that you can practice in free clinics and other charitable venues

    • Basic membership is free. Simply click on the links below to join the association and become involved with our online network of retired physicians.

    Volunteer Medicine

    We are building a database of volunteer opportunities for physicians and other healthcare practitioners for the US and around the world.

    Mentorship Exchange

    Online mentoring of practicing physicians. You can share your wisdom with those who are currently facing the challenges and issues so familiar to you.
