Mentorship Exchange-For Fellows and Residents


You are invited to join the free Mentorship Exchange of the National Association of Retired Physicians. This is an opportunity for you to ask questions to retired physicians-individuals who spent decades facing the same challenges and issues that you now face.

These retirees have a wealth of experience and wisdom-decades spent healing people. You'll have an opportunity to post questions and comments to the discussion and to yourself share whatever insights you may have.

These questions will typically be a point of departure for an ongoing discussion, generating a lot of back-and-forth discussion among the participants.

To join, simply click here and fill out the form. The discussion will take place entirely via e-mail. Members of the Mentorship Exchange can simply send a message to mentor@naorp.org. By sending your message to that address you will be sending it to the entire group.

You will receive messages via e-mail that are posted by other members. If you reply to a message, it will go to the entire group.

The forum will be monitored so that it can be restricted to legitimate postings.

Privacy Statement: The National Association of Retired Physicians will never share your e-mail address with any other person or entity. You will never receive unauthorized e-mail as a result of submitting your e-mail address.
